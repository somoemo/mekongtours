Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Luxury Cu Chi Tunnels & Mekong Delta Full Day

Located about 70 kilometers northwest of Ho Chi Minh City, the Cu Chi Tunnels are an elaborate network of underground tunnels used by the Viet Cong soldiers during the Vietnam War. On the other hand, the Mekong Delta is a vast maze of rivers, swamps, and islands, known for its rich biodiversity and unique way of life. Both these destinations offer a glimpse into Vietnam's history and culture, making it a must-visit for any traveler.

Exploring the Cu Chi Tunnels

As you reach the Cu Chi Tunnels, you will be amazed by the vastness of the underground network. The tunnels were used by the Viet Cong soldiers as hiding spots, supply routes, and living quarters during the Vietnam War. Your guide will take you through the tunnels, giving you a first-hand experience of the conditions the soldiers lived in. You will also have the opportunity to crawl through a section of the tunnels yourself, giving you a taste of the intense conditions the soldiers endured.

After exploring the tunnels, you will have the chance to try your hand at shooting at a shooting range, with a variety of weapons used during the war. This is an optional activity and can be skipped if you are not comfortable with it.
